About Me

Welcome to my art studio! I’m Khanaya Olivia Iriawan, a Visual Communication Design student at Unikom Bandung, and I’m dedicated to creating art that speaks to the heart. My journey began early, where creativity became my favorite way to express emotions and ideas.

I blend influences from nature, culture, and personal experiences to develop a style that's both bold and thoughtful. My goal? To create pieces that resonate, whether it’s a commissioned work or a personal project, sparking inspiration in everyone who views them.

Thank you for stopping by! I hope my art brings as much joy to you as it brings me to create.
