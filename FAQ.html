Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Will there be a difference in quality between the Normal, Cheap and Commercial packages?

No, the quality of the artwork is consistent across all packages. The difference between packages lies in the level of detail and features provided, such as the number of characters and the complexity of the background.

2. What if I need more revisions than included in my package?

You can request additional revisions for $5 each, beyond the revisions included in your package.

3. Can I add more characters to my illustration?

Yes, you can add more characters by requesting additional characters as an extra service for an additional fee of $3.45 per character.

4. What if I need the artwork for commercial use?

Commercial use licenses are available for an additional fee. Please check the package details or contact me for more information.

5. How long will it take to complete my commission?

1-2 weeks per piece. Time may vary depending on complexity and current workload. But I'll try my best; it'll be at least 7 days.

6. How do I place an order?

Just send me a message on my website or go to Fiverr to order. For communicating, I use Instagram.

7. What payment methods do you accept?

For Indonesian clients, I accept Bank BCA or DANA. For international clients, I accept PayPal.
